S'mores Pie

Description

A quick and easy tasty treat for the chocolate and pie lovers in your family.

Ingredients

  • 2 (3.9 ounce) packages instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 (10 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
  • 1 (8 ounce) container whipped topping, thawed

Directions

  1. Whisk 1 box pudding mix with 1/2 of the milk in a bowl until smooth, about 2 minutes. Repeat with second box of pudding mix and remaining milk in another bowl.
  2. Pour 1 bowl pudding into graham cracker crust. Put 1/2 of the marshmallows on the pudding. Top with remaining pudding, then layer with marshmallows. Put whipped topping over the marshmallows and level it out with a spatula.
  3. Chill in the refrigerator before serving, 1 to 2 hours.
